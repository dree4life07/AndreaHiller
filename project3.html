Deja Glow Spa

Anna Waddington, owner of Deja Glow Spa, exudes confidence in her craft and needed a logo express the same amount of confidence. Simple yet unique, my goal was to create a logo that made her clients feel glowing before they even stepped inside. Her various promotional cards were designed with a similar look and feel as the aesthetic of her spa.
