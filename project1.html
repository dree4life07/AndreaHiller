Speed Performance Training

Founder, Kyle Meadows, has been in the performance training business a long time and in 2020, hired me to bring his brand vision life. SPEED (scientific performance enhancing explosive development) embodies the motion of any athlete, with a focus on finding the pinacle of strength.

Kyle works with a diverse group of athletes young and old and needed a logo that was to the point and recognizable on merchandise from afar, yet approachable. I established brand guidelines with a vertical and horizontal version of the logo, as well as a logo mark that can stand on its own.
