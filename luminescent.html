Luminescent
Psychiatric Care

Claudia Crenshaw, owner, psychiatric care nurse and therapist, opened her own business during the year we all needed someone to talk to, 2020. The logo I created is based off of a personal experience she shared that moved her to open her business.

My intention was to invoke a sense of togetherness and strength in working through things while using a calming color palette and a classic, yet unique typeface. Her website is a simple single page, fully responsive design that has helped her business grow.
